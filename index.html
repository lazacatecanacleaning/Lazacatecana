Our Service Packages 🧹

Choose the package that best fits your home size and cleaning needs. All services are customizable upon request.

🏘️

1-2 Bed Maintenance Clean

**Summary:** Perfect for small apartments, condos, and bi-weekly clients. Focuses on essential upkeep, ensuring all highly-used areas are sanitized and tidy. Typically a 2-3 hour service.

*Includes standard dusting, light kitchen/bath cleanup, and floor care.*

🏡

Full House Deep Clean (The Reset)

**Summary:** Recommended for first-time clients or homes cleaned less than once a month. This is a top-to-bottom service that includes detailed attention to buildup, grout, baseboards, and hard-to-reach areas. Typically 4+ hours.

*Includes everything in Maintenance, plus intensive scrubbing and detail work.*

📦

Move In/Out Cleaning

**Summary:** A specialized service to prepare a property for sale or a new resident. This service is performed on empty homes and includes cleaning inside all cabinets, appliances, and all interior window glass. Typically 6+ hours.

*Includes all Deep Clean items, plus empty cabinets, oven, and fridge interior.*
